Roller Coaster Facts 2: The Longest Roller Coaster

Do you know the longest roller coaster in the world? It is situated at Nagashima Spa Land in Japan. This longest roller coaster has the track at 8133 feet. You have to ride the steel dragon 2000.

Roller Coaster Facts 3: The Tallest Roller Coaster

The tallest roller coaster in the world has the height at 465 feet. It is the Kingda Ka roller coaster. The location of this roller coaster is at Six Flags great Adventure Park.

Roller Coaster Facts 8: The First Steel Roller Coaster

The first roller coaster made of steel was the Matterhorn bobsled roller coaster. It was the first tubular steel roller coaster ever made in 1959. The roller coaster was created for Disneyland in Anaheim, California.
